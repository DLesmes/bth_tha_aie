# Holistic Enterprise Cost Model Report
### Target Environment: Secure Agentic RAG Deployment (500–2,000 Employees)
**Profile Focus:** Realistic Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) — 4-Week MVP to Production

---

## 1. Assumptions & Baseline Mathematics
To establish realistic costs, the model is anchored on the following explicit enterprise metrics:
* **Company Size:** 500–2,000 employees (Targeting ~1,000 active profile seats).
* **Knowledge Store Size:** 20,000 corporate documents (compliance, policies, internal documentation).
* **Chunking Density:** Averaging 5 chunks per document = **100,000 total structural text chunks**.
* **Token Sizing:** ~300 words per chunk $\approx$ 400 tokens per chunk. Total knowledge store volume $\approx$ **40 Million tokens**.
* **Query Volume (Tier 1 Baseline):** 1,000 queries per business day $\approx$ **22,000 queries per month**.

---

## 2. One-Time Build Costs
Moving the architecture from conceptual diagram to live enterprise execution involves engineering labor, environment provisioning, and data ingestion/vectorization.

### A. Engineering Labor Hours
Estimated implementation timeline of 4 weeks leveraging a lean, senior technical team:
* **Senior AI/ML Engineer (LangGraph & FGA Logic):** 120 hours @ $130/hr = **$15,600**
* **DevOps / Cloud Platform Engineer (Azure & CI/CD Mesh):** 80 hours @ $120/hr = **$9,600**
* **QA / Security Compliance Testing:** 40 hours @ $90/hr = **$3,600**

### B. Infrastructure Setup & Environment Sandbox
* Initial deployment automated via Terraform/ARM templates, sandbox verification testing pipeline configurations, and Auth0 FGA relation store setup: **$150.00** (flat cloud resource testing budget).

### C. Initial Data Ingestion & Indexing Pipeline
* **Document Parsing Compute:** Handled entirely inside the local `uv` container workflow during initialization = **$0.00**
* **Vector Model Processing (OpenAI text-embedding-3-large):** Processing 40 Million tokens to generate 2,048-dimension embeddings:
  $$\text{40M tokens} \times \frac{\$0.13}{\text{1M tokens}} = \mathbf{\$5.20}$$

**Total One-Time Build Subtotal: $28,955.20**

* **The Front Agent Multiplier Effect:** The architecture's single greatest cost shield is the **Front Agent Node**. By instantly blocking off-topic queries deterministically, it bypasses Weaviate, Auth0 FGA, and the LLM completely, locking down predictable infrastructure costs even under heavy employee abuse.
